Our Story

Welcome to Tailored Donuts, where every bite is a delightful adventure! Picture this: fluffy clouds of goodness, swirling with flavors that dance on your taste buds. It all began in 2005, when our founder, Taylor, discovered a magical passion for crafting mouthwatering desserts while baking alongside her family. From that moment, our mission was clear: to revolutionize the world of donuts with a dash of fun and a sprinkle of health-conscious joy!

At Tailored Donuts, we're all about crafting treats that are not just delicious, but also good for you. We handpick only the finest organic, unprocessed ingredients, ensuring that each bite is a harmonious blend of satisfaction and nourishment. No more sugar crashes – just guilt-free indulgence and pure joy in every flavor-packed bite!

Located in the heart of Murfreesboro, Tennessee, we're not just a bakery; we're your go-to destination for special events and pop-up donut delights. Whether you're celebrating life's milestones or simply craving a scrumptious treat, our wholesome creations are here to elevate your experience and make every moment sweeter.

Join us on a flavorful journey where taste meets wellness, and let's make every bite an unforgettable celebration – together at Tailored Donuts!
